Equilibrium

EQUILIBRIUM APP

   The emergence of technology in mental health services, such as teletherapy, has paved the way for increased accessibility and convenience in seeking support for emotional well-being. This opportunity aligns closely with the mission of Equilibrium, an innovative app that is crafted to aid parents in fostering emotionally resilient children with secure attachment styles.
   Equilibrium offers a range of features that empower parents to recalibrate their behavior and interactions with their children, fostering secure attachment bonds. By leveraging technology, parents can access these resources from the comfort of their own home, eliminating barriers, such as distance or mobility issues, that may have obstructed their ability to seek traditional support services.

   The Equilibrium app was created is to assist parents in raising emotionally balanced children, with a healthy attachment style. It has several different options to help the parents to recalibrate their behavior in order to create a secure attachment style: educational content, self-assessment tools, guided reflection exercises, interactive coaching, mindfulness techniques,...

Anxious attachment signs:
- fear
- not controlling negative emotions
- anxiety
- seeking reassurance
- difficulty trusting
- clingy
- highly emotionally reactive

Secured attachment signs:
- separates from parents
- seeks comfort from parents when frightened
- greets return of parents with positive emotions
- prefers parents to strangers
- have trusting, lasting relationships
- share feelings with partners and friends
- seek out social support

Avoidant attachment signs:
- may avoid parents
- do not seek much contact or comfort from parents
- show little or no preference for parents over strangers
- may have problems with intimacy
- invest little emotion in social and romantic relationships
- unwilling or unable to share thoughts or feelings with others

Disorganized attachment signs:
- show a mixture of avoidant and resistant behavior
- may seem dazed, confused, or apprehensive
- may take on a parental role
- some children may act as a caregiver toward the parents
- feels both comforted and frightened by the parent
- may push the other person away

   The Equilibrium app is a web-based software, designed for end-users, the parents.
   It would use: various sensors (microphones, cameras, or wearable devices, audio or video). The app may rely on cloud infrastructure for data storage, processing, and analysis, because of the large volume of data collected from multiple users.
